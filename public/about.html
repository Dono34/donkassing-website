About

The story behind the results

25+ years of turning strategy into execution — and ideas into outcomes.

Don E. Kassing is an accomplished executive leader with over 25 years of C-level experience spanning operations, finance, strategic planning, and organizational transformation.


His career arc tells a story of relentless growth — from financial analyst at Honeywell, where he was promoted 9 times in 10 years, to CEO of JW Aluminum, where he grew the company from $230MM to $490MM in revenue and orchestrated a $350MM sale that delivered a 347% ROI for investors.


Today, Don runs three ventures: Aspire2 Consulting (strategic consulting), aCom Success Partners (Amazon ecommerce management), and Aspire2 Automate (AI & automation consulting). He brings the same operational rigor and strategic thinking that drove results at Fortune 100 companies to entrepreneurs and growth-stage businesses.


A Six Sigma Master Black Belt with an MBA from Washington University and a BA from Brown University, Don combines analytical depth with practical execution. He's raised over $900MM in capital, led teams of 1,000+, and spent 5 years as an expatriate leading European operations.
